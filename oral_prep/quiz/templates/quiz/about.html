About

The Oral Prep Tool has been created to collect private pilot oral prep question as a tool for students (and instructors) to help drill private and commercial pilot candidates on oral exam questions. If the "private" option is selected then all commercial questions are excluded, and if "commercial" is selected you get a mix of both.

This site is a rewrite of the excellent Oral Prep Tool built by Dwight Frye.

The original site was dedicated to Betsy McCracken in honor of her dedication as a flight instructor at the Wings of Carolina Flying Club. Her private pilot oral prep sessions were downright legendary. It was said, honestly, that if you could get past Betsy, there wasn't a DPE out there with the temerity to question your knowledge.
